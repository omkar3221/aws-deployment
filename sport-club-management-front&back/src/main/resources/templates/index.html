SPORTS CLUB

Every dream needs to be driven by passion to make it come true. Sports Club is one such dream, formed by keeping sheer passion for the gentleman's game-Cricket. Sports Club was conceived by a group of people headed by Chinubhai Chimanbhai; the then Mayor and leading businessman from Mumbai in 1965. "Sports Club" is a part of the Association which evolved with the objective of creating Sports & Recreational facilities such as Football, Cricket, Skating, Karate, Squash, Badminton, Lawn Tennis, Table Tennis, Snooker/Billiards, Bridge Room, Swimming, Scuba Diving, Gymnasium, Spa (Steam/Sauna/Jacuzzi/Massage), Zumba Fitness, Yoga etc.

History

The Sports Club gets its flamboyant and elegant design in the heart of city from the renowned architect Charles Correa who also happens to be the designer of the soulful Gandhi Ashram. With a beautiful design and sprawling space; Sports Club has gradually spread its branches for other sports and has become an epitome of entertainment and leisure in the thriving city of Mumbai. Today, Sports Club is one of the most regal clubs of Mumbai and renowned for its state of the art amenities and splendid aura.

Rules And Regulations

1.Outside Food and Beverages including Alcoholic Beverages and other consumables are not permitted to be brought into the Club.
2.All Members shall ensure that they leave the Club premises before the closing time. The Club staff/security has been instructed to report the names of those Members who contravene these Rules.
3.Entry into the Club is not permitted in Night Suits, Lungis or loose Pyjamas. National dress suitably pressed may be allowed.
4.Rubber/Hawaii chappals or Bathroom slippers are not permitted anywhere, in the Club except around the swimming pool area.
5.Any change, such as address, phone nos. mobile nos. of all family members, e-mail addresses, birth, death, marriage, divorce, adoption or any other change in a Member's family must be communicated to the Club with the relevant documents, within three months of the occurrence.
6.ROLLER SKATES, ETC. ARE NOT PERMITTED IN THE CLUB PREMISES. WHEEL CHAIRS WHERE REQUIRED ARE PERMITTED. HOWEVER, MEMBERS MAY PARK THEIR BI- CYCLES IN THE AREAS DESIGNATED BY THE MANAGING COMMITTEE FROM TIME TO TIME & ROLLER SKATES SHALL ONLY BE PERMITTED DURING SKATING CLASSES HELD IN THE CLUB PREMISES.
